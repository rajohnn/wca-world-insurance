FREIGHT SERVICES LIABILITY

The single most important protection for your business

Tailored to the unique needs of freight forwarders and customs brokers



Our FSL or Freight Services Liability policy combines Errors & Omissions and Legal Liability insurance to protect your company against potentially devastating loses.

Examples of errors & omissions claims

  • Delay caused by negligence
  • Failure to follow instructions
  • Mistakes in preparation of documents
  • Improper Release without
  • Original Bill of Lading
  • Fines & Penalties
  • Failure to properly or promptly clear goods
  • Incorrect import product classification
  • Negligent selection of a carrier or third party
  • Not providing proper cargo insurance

Examples of cargo liability claims

  • Cargo damage of all kinds while moving under your house bills of lading
  • Loss or damage to cargo while at your warehouse or agents warehouse
  • Demurrage on cargo uncollected at destination
  • General Average
  • Consequential Loss
  • Duty
  • Temporary Storage Extension
  • Accidental Delay in Delivery
  • Warehouse Legal Liability
